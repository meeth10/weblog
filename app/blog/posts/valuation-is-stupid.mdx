I have always loved buying things at bargain. To bargain you would need to know the true value of it. Anyone who has tried to build or value a Stock would have heard of the term “Intrinsic Valuation” and with that comes a profound Man - Aswath Damodaran!

I at least have watched his lecture series 4 times and countlessly tried valuing these companies and for some reason I still can’t get these stock at bargain. I thought hard and felt I wasn’t meant for trade or valuing companies.

Till I understood something; THE MARKET IS STUPID !! THE INVESTORS ARE STUPID !! blame law of large numbers for it.

The idea was not to call everyone morons (maybe it was) rather it’s to say sometimes what’s seen as a bargain was always a bargain; Take ONGC has never trade more than it’s intrinsic value. Same goes for Coal India and I can go on but then I can list a dozen more that have never trade below their Intrinsic Value not because they were so good but because the carried names that could never produce a loss? Seriously that is the reason given more often than not !!

Still blame me for calling everyone stupid? I have theory that sits me well; Never trade with capital you don’t have( which you can’t afford to lose) on companies you don’t feel right and finally never rethink a trade you made.
