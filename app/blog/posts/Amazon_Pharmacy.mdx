Introduction:

Amazon pharmacy is primarily an Online business, allowing users to order and receive prescriptions and over the top medication directly to their home. Amazon has very few retail stores. The figures of YOY growth and operation margin are not known, Overall pharmacies have seen a decline in gross margins. According to certain sources Amazon pharmacy only contributes 0.003 % to Amazon's Revenue(Pharmacy has revenue of 1.8 B in 2024 while amazon has 637B in revenue). Currently Amazon Pharmacy holds around 0.3% of the segment which is growing at the rate of 4% in the USA where they are primarily situated.


Analysis:-

Porters 5 Forces -
a) Threat of New Entrant - LOW - The pharmacy industry is a very capital intensive business with finding right storing facilities and also presents high regulatory requirements. Thus the threat of someone new coming and disrupting would be low as the regulatory requirements wouldnt allow it.

b) Power OF Buyer - HIGH - To begin with there are very few drug manufactures and the switching cost is so high that often buyers present a huge problem for profitability.

c) Power Of Supplier - HIGH - Buyers are getting more cost aware and are constantly searching for cheaper alternatives. Because they are so Price sensitive there is a choke hold put on the business to keep prices as real as possible.

d) Threat of Substitue - MODERATE - There aint true substitutes for the business but Physical Pharmacies and Cost Plug Drugs are potential threats to business.

e) Competitive Rivarly - HIGH - CVS, Walgreens, Rite Aid and other platforms are constantly competing with each other and all of them compete over price causing it to become a zero sum game.


Key Competitors -
a) CVS - CVS is not just the most trusted drug seller but also a vertically integrated healthcare giant. They own several retail locations, walk in clinics and also have thier own insurance.

b) WallGreens Boots Alliance - They are both physical retailers and a digital retailer, Investing heavy on digital presence and telehealth partnerships.

c) GoodRx - Discount heavy price transparent platform. Users often rely on them for cheap medicines.

d) Cost Plug Drug Company - focused on generic medicine, to provide even cheaper medicine than GoodRx tho they are heavy on the online presence as well The Story is simple despite this being a cost sensitive segment Companies play to small variations and different angles, If amazon has to make a presence felt it needs to find a niche or comptieve with prices.


SWOT -

Strength -

1) Amazon Brand Name - Integrating it with prime membership and Amzons brand provides more trust.

2) Seamless Integration into Logistics - Using the vast Amazon Logistics infrastructure to provide efficient medicine delivery.

3) Technology Integration - Using Alexa to order your medicine.


Weakness -

1) Limited Physical Presence -Medicine is still something people like buying physically which hurts amazon as they have very limized presence.

2) Market Perception - Amazon is largely viewed as ecommerce giant. People do not trust them with sensitive healthcare medicine.

Opportunity -

1) Expansion into Cronic space -Moving away from generic medicine and focusing on more profitable cronics disease healthcare space.

2) International Markets -While the USA might be fiercely competitive, multiple markets exist that can use Amazon Pharmacy.

3) Partnership and Integration - Integration in the vertical domain or partnering in the same domain would be a great way to build market presence something like P and G.

Threats -

1) Intense Competition - CVS and Walgreens are two giants that would provide intense competition in any space Amazon thinks to enter due to their vast presence.

2) HealtCare regulations - Any Regulations place could create problems to an upcomer which is amazon.

Growth Opportunities -

Well the first thing going for amazon is the name and logistics. Amazon is a synonym with trust and has been around for a long time, the other thing amazon has been good at is Logisitcs. Clearly the american market is very competitive and the profitability choke put on amazon from all sides makes it hard for it to survive. So what now?
In my perspective if Amazon has to grow and make it another AWS there are few changes they shall make:-

1) Blue Ocean Strategy - Do not just sell drugs, have an ecosystem for both doctors and clients. Use Alexa for booking appointments (say) then based on prescription at it to your amazon pharmacy order / Put in your cart of amazon. Have the data of all patients stored on AWS cloud and use Ai to smartly book their appointments and reschedule patients based off priorirty. CVS and other giants can copy or follow but do they have the logistics cash and ecosystem amazon does?

2) Move out of the USA - Amazon has been playing in a very competitive market where reaching the top is nearly impossible. The idea is not bad of integrating OTC medicines to Amazon's logistics, it's just that there are better markets where they shall think to play.

3) Merger - Amazon can acquire one of the giants like they did with whole foods to provide them some breathing space in terms of competitive rivals and get experience in the form of acquisition which can fuel their growth.

4) Closing the Business - This is a bad segment to enter and fight for profitability, started in 2020 and producing just a revenue of 2 B which is 0.003 % speaks to how this is not a business that's generating revenue rather a hobby business.

5) Rare Medicine - Not a large market but capturing this niche market where getting rare drugs would help it capture a segment of the market which isnt tapped yet.
